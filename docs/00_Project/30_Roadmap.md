# Roadmap Pengembangan

Tim 4 orang, target MVP siap soft launch dalam 6 sprint (12 minggu). Urutan implementasi mengikuti 10 fase pengembangan dari arsitektur.

## 6 Sprint (12 Minggu)

- **Sprint 1 — Foundation & Authentication**: setup Appwrite, auth, role system (UMKM/Creator/Admin), wallet otomatis saat registrasi, upload avatar/media, landing + login/register/onboarding.
- **Sprint 2 — Creator Profile & Rate Card**: edit profil creator, social account, niche, portfolio, rate card, request verifikasi; UMKM creator discovery (search + filter).
- **Sprint 3 — Campaign Module (Core)**: create campaign + upload raw video, AI Brief Assistant, budget setup; creator browse/claim/submit; status campaign & submission.
- **Sprint 4 — Order, Offer & Escrow**: chat room, custom offer, accept offer, order creation, escrow, revision & delivery system; wallet deposit/withdraw/history.
- **Sprint 5 — AI Fraud & Analytics**: verifikasi URL submission, view tracking, deteksi pola mencurigakan, fraud score; analytics UMKM & creator.
- **Sprint 6 — Admin Panel & Production Ready**: user/campaign/order management, withdrawal approval, dispute resolution, fraud monitoring; notification, email, error logging, audit log, security review, optimasi performa.

## 10 Fase Implementasi (Urutan Coding)

1. Authentication
2. Creator Profile + Rate Card
3. Campaign (Viral / PPV)
4. Submission
5. Order + Escrow
6. Wallet
7. Notification
8. AI Brief Generator
9. Fraud Detection
10. Admin Dashboard

## Prioritas

- **P0 (Wajib saat soft launch)**: Authentication, Creator Profile, Rate Card, Campaign, Submission, Wallet, Escrow, Admin Panel, AI Brief Assistant, Basic Fraud Detection.
- **P1 (Setelah launch)**: Recommendation AI, Smart Creator Matching, Advanced Analytics, Auto Moderation, Push Notification, Referral Program.
- **P2 (Growth stage)**: Mobile App, Creator Ranking, Affiliate System, Agency Dashboard, Regional Expansion.
